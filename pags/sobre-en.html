About the Project

Our Purpose

This project, "Pioneering Minds," is our contribution as students from Etec Sebrae. Although inspired by the foundational work "História da Computação" by Raul Sidnei Wazlawick, our goal was not just to retell history, but to expand upon it.

We believe the best way to learn is by interacting. Therefore, we created a digital platform that invites you to explore this journey through articles, a visual timeline, an interactive quiz, and 3D models of the inventions that shaped our world.

Our Inspiration: A Reinterpretation

Our proposal was to go beyond the text, transforming fundamental concepts into experiences. Below, we compare the original book cover with our reinterpretation, which seeks to capture the "steampunk" aesthetic and the mechanical essence of the first inventors.
