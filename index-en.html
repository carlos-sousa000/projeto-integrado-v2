Notable People

Ada Lovelace

Considered the world's first programmer. Her visionary algorithms for the Analytical Engine laid the foundations of modern computing.

Charles Babbage

Known as the "father of the computer", he conceived the Analytical Engine — a visionary design that anticipates modern computers.

Joseph Henry

An American scientist and inventor who contributed to the development of electromagnetism. His work influenced the telegraph and electric motors.
